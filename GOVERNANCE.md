# VEIP Standards Council Charter

Version 1.0 (Draft)

## I. Purpose

The VEIP Standards Council governs the evolution, integrity, and interoperability of the Veraxis Execution Integrity Protocol (VEIP).

The Council exists to:

- Maintain the formal VEIP specification
- Preserve deterministic execution integrity principles
- Prevent fragmentation of supervisory semantics
- Provide a stable control-plane standard for autonomous systems
